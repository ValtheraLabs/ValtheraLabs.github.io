## Assets

- The ValtheraLabs geometric emblem and wordmark are the company marks.
- Preserve clear space, proportions, color contrast, and SVG geometry.
- `app/icon.svg` is the favicon and manifest icon.
- `public/x-banner.png` is the current social preview image.
- `public/brand.html` is an illustrative brand specimen. It must not contain fabricated company metrics, addresses, clients, or credentials.

## Navigation and attribution

- The personal site presents ValtheraLabs as the company and ValtheraSwap as its product or ecosystem project.
- The company site links to Dionis Markov where founder context is useful and to ValtheraSwap as a featured product.
- ValtheraSwap links back to ValtheraLabs and labels application, documentation, mainnet, and testnet destinations accurately.
- Footers use the owning identity. Cross-site links must use HTTPS. Do not imply audits, partnerships, clients, scale, or performance without evidence.
